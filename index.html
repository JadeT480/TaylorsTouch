What is Seated Acupressure?

Seated Acupressure is a Meridian based therapy, that requires applying pressure to various acupressure points across the shoulders, back, arms, hands, neck, head, and legs. This approach utilizes gentle methods like pushing, pulling, rocking, shaking, stretching, and mobilization.
